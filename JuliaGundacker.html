Do plants like music?

Who doesn't, as long as it's good music!
Believe it or not, plant growth is affected by music. To plants, it is not primarly important if you go for Jazz, Rock or Pop music - they just love to vibe along! The vibrations created by the sound waves matter to them. In simple terms, the vibrations produce movement in the plant cells, which stimulates the plant to produce more nutrients. Which kind of music affects the growth of different kinds of plants best, is not scientifically proven (yet). Find out more on the impact of music on plant growth here.

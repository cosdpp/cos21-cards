Anna's holy hummus

The most delicious hummus is wonderfully creamy and at the same time light and airy. Forget the purchased one. With this recipe you can easily make your own hummus and make it creamy and fluffy like never before. You don't need much more than chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, and spices.

Ingredients


  • Chickpeas and its water
  • Lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Tahini
  • Ice cold water
  • Cumin
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Drain the canned chickpeas through a sieve. Collect 50 ml of chickpea water and keep a few chickpeas as decoration. Put everything aside.

  2. Put the juice of one lemon, the clove of garlic and 1/2 teaspoon salt in the blender and blend until smooth.

  3. In the second step, put the 120 g tahini in the mixer and mix thoroughly. Slowly pour the cold water and the chickpea water into the running knife. A light, fluffy mass should result.

  4. In the last step, add the chickpeas, 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil and 1/2 teaspoon of cumin and mix for at least 3 minutes until a creamy, tender mixture is formed. Season to taste with salt and lemon juice. If the hummus is too thick, mix in some more cold water.

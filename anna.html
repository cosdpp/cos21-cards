Anna's holy hummus

The most delicious hummus is wonderfully creamy and at the same time light and airy. Forget the purchased one. With this recipe you can easily make your own hummus and make it creamy and fluffy like never before. You don't need much more than chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, and spices.

Ingredients

  •  Canned chickpeas
  •  Lemon juice
  •  Salt
  •  Garlic
  •  Tahini
  •  Ice cold water
  •  Cumin
  •  Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Drain the canned chickpeas through a sieve – continue working with 265 g of drained chickpeas. Collect 50 ml of chickpea water and keep a few chickpeas as decoration. Put everything aside.

  2. Put the juice of one lemon, the clove of garlic and 1/2 teaspoon salt in the blender and blend until smooth.

  3. Put the 100 g Tahini (best: from Koska) in the mixer and mix thoroughly. Slowly pour the cold water and the chickpea water into the running knife. A light, fluffy mass should result.

  4. In the last step, add the chickpeas, 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil (can be left out if you want to decrease fat) and 1/2 teaspoon of cumin and mix for at least 3 minutes until a creamy, tender mixture is formed. Season to taste with salt and lemon juice. If the hummus is too thick, mix in some more cold water.

Voilá! To serve, drizzle the hummus with olive oil and decorate with toppings (chickpeas, sesame, herbs). Crackers, pita bread or vegetable sticks go well with it.
